\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Draw a cylinder using Frame\+Resources. 

We define a cylinder by specifying its bottom and top radii, its height, and the slice and stack count. We break the cylinder into three parts\+: 1) the side geometry 2) the top cap geometry 3) the bottom cap geometry. ~\newline


Controls\+: Hold down \textquotesingle{}1\textquotesingle{} key to view scene in wireframe mode. Hold the left mouse button down and move the mouse to rotate. Hold the right mouse button down and move the mouse to zoom in and out.
